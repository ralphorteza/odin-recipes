Yakisoba

A popular stir-fry noodles seen in your weeb animes

Recipe

  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 3 shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 green onions
  • 4 cabbage leaves
  • 3/4lb sliced pork belly
  • vegetable oil
  • 3 pkg akisoba noodles
  • black pepper
  • 4-6 Tbsp yakisoba sauce

Steps

  1. Gather all vegetables and mushrooms and slice them all into strips
  2. Slice meat into 1 inch pieces
  3. Cook yakisoba noodles in a boiling pot for 3-5 minutes; Strain an rinse in a strainer and set aside
  4. Heat flat griddle or wok to medium high heat and thinly coat the surface with oil
  5. Cook the meat on the surface until meat is no longer pink; Stir in carrots and onions and cook for 1-2 minutes
  6. Add in cabbage and cook until tender; Lastly add in the mushrooms and green onions and cook for an additional 1 minute
  7. Run noodles through hot water and shake and strain off excess water; Stir in the noodles fractionally while stir-frying all the ingredients until all are throughly mixed together
  8. Mix in the yakisoba sauce and black pepper until everything is throughly coated
  9. Transfer yakisoba off the cooking surface and unto a tray
  10. Serve and enjoy
