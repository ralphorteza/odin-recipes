Tenzaru Soba

A buckwheat noodle meal enjoyed on cool summer evenings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mentsuyu dipping sauce
  • chopped green onions
  • chopped daikon
  • wasabi
  • 5 to 6 pieces tempura vegetables
  • 2 to 3 pieces tempura shrimp
  • 8 ounces soba uckwheat noodles

Steps

  1. Prepare the mentsuyu dipping sauce, then let chill in the refrigerator for 30 min
  2. Slice green onions and grate daikon
  3. Boil noodles for 3 minutes, then quickly cool in an ice bath; Strain afterwards
  4. Fry vegetable and shrimp tempura, then set aside
  5. Plate soba noodles and garnish with green onions,daikon, and wasabi; Set tempura on separate dishplate and serve with the dipping sauce
