pho

A delicatable vietnamese noodle soup that rivals ramen

Ingredients

broth
  • beef knuckles or leg bones
  • cold water
  • onions
  • fish sauce
  • garlic cloves
  • salt
Assembly
  • bahn pho noodles
  • raw thin slice sirloin steak
  • thinly sliced onions
  • chopped cilantro
Table Garnish
  • basil leaves
  • bean sprouts
  • lime wedges
  • hoisin sauce
  • fish sauce

Steps

Broth
  1. In a large pot, add beef bones and water; Bring to a boil, then remove any scum the floats atop
  2. Bring broth into a parboil and add the rest of the broth ingredients
  3. Bring boil to a simmer for 3-5 hours and occassionally removing scum
  4. With a catch pot, strain broth through a strainer to remove the last impurities
Assembly
  1. Bring a water pot to a boil and place noodles for 3-5 minutes
  2. Strain noodles and rinse with cold water; place noodles in a serving bowl
  3. Arrange the meat, onions, and cilantro ontop of the noodles
  4. Add broth onto the bowl
Serving
  1. Serve noodle soup with the given and optional garnishes and enjoy
